About Nicaela

I connect people to nature.
How I do it, generally: by making them care. If they care, the facts will stick.
How I do it, specifically: by teaching, drawing, writing, or a combination.

I currently work for the Utah Division of Wildlife Resources as a teacher-naturalist at Hardware Ranch Wildlife Management Area.

I teach a lot. Please read about some of the educational programs I’ve delivered, designed and coordinated.

I’m a bit quiet. I have no social media presence. I think that’s just fine. Feel free to contact me via email.
